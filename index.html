AI for your org

Workshops that prepare your team to use AI safely.

Adoption fails when people don’t know what’s allowed, what’s risky, or how to use AI in real workflows. We train teams with a simple rule: move fast, protect trust.

  • Executive AI literacy (decisions, limits, failure modes)
  • Secure GenAI usage (data rules, prompt hygiene)
  • Use-case selection (ROI + feasibility + risk)
  • Human factors & change management (reduce fear, prevent shadow AI)
  • Operating model (roles, approvals, escalation)
  • Workshop outputs: prioritized backlog + adoption guidelines
AI governance

Governance that enables delivery — and stands up to scrutiny.

We build governance that executives can run and auditors can validate. It’s clear, minimal, and built for regulated / safety-critical environments.

  • AI policy (what’s allowed / reviewed / prohibited)
  • Governance framework creation (roles, workflows)
  • Risk assessments (privacy, security, safety, bias)
  • Model inventory + lifecycle (owners, data, approvals)
  • Vendor governance (controls, contracts, evidence)
  • Audit readiness (documentation, logs, reviews)
